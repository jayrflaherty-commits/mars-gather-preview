Pet Parent Archetypes
🏋️ Performance Optimizers (28%)

Research-driven. Read ingredients obsessively. Willing to pay 2× for proven nutrition. Over-index on fresh/raw brands.

🛋️ Comfort Nurturers (33%)

Treats = love language. Prioritize taste and their pet's excitement. Strong loyalty once they find "the one." Mars's core audience.

💰 Budget Pragmatists (22%)

Value-driven but not careless. Want "good enough" nutrition at accessible prices. Pedigree's sweet spot but losing share to Costco private label.

😰 Health-Anxious (17%)

Triggered by pet health scares or vet recommendations. Will switch brands overnight. Over-trust DTC brands with "clinical" messaging.
